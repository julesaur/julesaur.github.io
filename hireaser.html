Hi-Polymer Eraser |Minecraft & 3D Print| 2020

I went on Minecraft in creative mode to build a Hi-Polymer Eraser. This is the only eraser that could completely erase my dark handwriting since I tend to make a lot of mistakes. Also, this symbolizes as erasing my past from embarrassing or regretful moments. I hope I can continue writing a meaningful future without erasing too many regretful memories. The eraser may be small, but it has the tool to wipe everything I command.

Friendship Bracelets Story

I handmade these friendship bracelets for my dearest friends whom I treasure during my lifetime. After careful consideration, I hope we can continue being everlasting friends.

Stacked Timeline

There are 8 bracelets total and each color represents my friends' favorite color. I stacked the bracelets in the order from bottom to top of how I met them for the first time.



Flower of Trust

My bracelet is in the center whereas my friends' bracelet are the petals. Each petal are chosen that I reconnect with during a busy schedule.



Meeting my Behalf

My old friendship bracelet since the 7th grade (left) and my newest one (right) is in a heart shape to symbolize the past and future.

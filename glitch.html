Flower Glitches Collage
|Text Editor & Audacity| 2019

These are my series of flowers. I used Text Editor to make a GLITCH version and Audacity to make an AUDIO version. I love flowers and it is often seen as “beautiful” or “pretty” on the outside. I decided to glitch effect to reveal their ugly side as well. To me, every type of flower has its insecurity like people too. Having beauty is a shield to protect your darkest deepest secrets.

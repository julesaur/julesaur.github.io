Net Art:Spaced Out |HTML & CSS| 2019

(Desktop Recommended if play ) Story: Spaced Out is a fictional choice story game that takes the audience on a “Y/N” trip to space for a getaway trip from the earth. Y/N navigates through their choices to discover a supernatural power.

This Net Art was an inspiration from a video I watched recently about a woman who wanted to live till 38 because she couldn't find happiness or love yet. I can relate to her story because as I get older, I feel like I was missing out on stuff, and I’m not sure I want to repeat the same things over and over again with school or work. My expressions of feeling done with life are not noticeable, so instead, I hide it with a smile.

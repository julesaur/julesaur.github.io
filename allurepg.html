Allure |TouchDesigner & SNOW| 2020

I used Touch Designer for mixing and glitching from a video I filmed of NCT 127 concert singing, "Highway to Heaven". I then added music audio into PremierPro and included transitions and filters in the mobile app SNOW.

I was aiming for a hypnotic effect like the black and white swirls, but with aesthetic color tones. The idea of Allure is that the audience will be enhanced and mesmerize with illusionary colors and lofi music tempo. Safe to say it does not control people, but just staring at the text, “Allure” glitching in and out is addicting enough to watch the whole video.
